Breakfast Club Bagel

Salty, smoky anc creamy this made-in-minutes toasted bagel with cream cheese, smoked salmon and avocado is ideal for Sunday brunch.

Ingredients

  • 50g Cream Cheese
  • 2 Bagels (halved and lightly toasted)
  • 100g Smoked Salmon (cut into slices)
  • 1 Avocado (sliced)
  • 1/2 Lemon (cut into wedges)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Pinch of Cayenne Pepper
